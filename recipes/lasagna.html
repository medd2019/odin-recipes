Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a basic recipe on how to make a Lasagna which is an Italian pasta dish that involves layering a meat bolognese sauce with layers of pasta and cheese.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 40 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasanga noodles
  • 2.5 cups of mozarella cheese

Steps

Prep Work

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out the remaining ingredients.

Making the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat the olive oil over medium heat then add the diced onions. Let them soften for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and increase the heat a bit. Use a spatula to break up the meat after a few minutes once browing on a side has occured. 8-10 minutes then add garlic
  3. Add the chicken broth and use a spatula to clean the bottom and sides of the pot. Add the marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce. Bring to a boil then reduce to a simmer. Cover partially and let the sauce simmer gently.

Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Boil a large pot of salted pasta water for the lasagna noodles. Once it reaches a boil add the noodles and cook until al dente according to the package instructions. Gently stir with a spoon throughout. Once cooked drain and rinse with cold water.
  3. Lay the cooked noodles on parchment paper while you begin assembling the lasagna.

Assemble

  1. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce at the bottom of the dish. Next add 4 lasanga noodles.
  2. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by about 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  3. Repeat these steps until you use up all of the noodles and meat sauce. About 3 layers.
  4. Top with 2 cups of mozarella cheese
  5. Cover with non stick aluminum foil

Bake

  1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes.
  2. Remove and garnish with fresh parsley. Let it sit for 15 mins prior to serving and enjoy!
